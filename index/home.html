Our Why: Market Insights

At Openhouse, we believe that the future is Africa. We challenge the status quo by positioning Africa as the next frontier for investment and innovation in real estate. Openhouse strives to break down the silos that hinder growth and collaboration in the African real estate market.

KEY CHALLENGES AND OPPORTIONITIES:
  • Growing Middle Class:The middle-class population in Africa will reach 1.1 billion (42% of the total population) by 2060 - United Nations.
  • Silos: Lack of collaboration and transparency in the real estate market increases costs and reduces efficiency.
  • Opaque:The market suffers from inadequate data, making informed investment decisions difficult.
Openhouse aims to bridge these gaps by fostering collaboration, transparency, and innovation in the African real estate sector.
